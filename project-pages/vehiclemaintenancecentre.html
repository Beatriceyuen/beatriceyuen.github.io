Vehicle Maintenance Centre
2019 / Kwai Chung, Hong Kong / 6,500 sqm
Project Details
A new technical service centre is one of the first in Asia to be certified as an Officina brand Classiche Centre. Nestled in an industrial area in Kwai Chung, the 6,500 sqm space is home to some of the most advanced equipment and facilities in the automotive realm.
One of the most challenging parts of this project is the custom made linear luminaire. The custom-made luminaire has a slim and modern designwith 39W 4200 lumens high output and an integrated LED transformer. The products reliability is important; especially the linear lighting fittings were installed at 4.5 meters ceiling height. I followed the different stages of this project, from the luminaire design, lux level calculation in Dialux EVO to the project handed over.
