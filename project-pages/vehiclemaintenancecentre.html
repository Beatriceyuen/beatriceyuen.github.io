Vehicle Maintenance Centre
2019 / Kwai Chung, Hong Kong / 6,500 sqm
Project Details
Client: Swire Properties
Architect: LWK + Partners
Lighting Consultant : Arup Hong Kong
More than 150 international brands and lifestyle brands are set to open their first Hong Kong outlet store at the premium outlet shopping complex, located at Lantau, near Hong Kong International Airport.
In the lighting design stage, I assisted the lighting design team of Arup Hong Kong in selecting the appropriate luminaires to achieve their concept. Most of the luminaires in this project, such as LED strips and LED downlights, are using LEDCO products in different outputs. Working as Project Manager, I communicated and followed up with the manufacturer to ensure the product’s quality and delivery that fulfilling the project schedule and requirment.
